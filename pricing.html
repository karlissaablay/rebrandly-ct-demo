Frequently asked questions

What counts as a tracked click?

A tracked click is any click on a link that has conversion tracking enabled. Clicks on links without tracking enabled don't count toward your limit.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.

What's an attribution window?

The time period after a click during which a conversion can be attributed to that click. A 30-day window means conversions up to 30 days after the click are counted.

Do I need a developer to set up?

For basic setup, no. You just paste one code snippet into your website header. Our wizard guides you through every step in about 15 minutes.
